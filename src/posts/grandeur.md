## Delusions of Grandeur
See here's the thing. It feels very motivating to feel that you're going to be the best rapper of all time, and in my heart of hearts it feels true. But then you start looking at the numbers. And trust me, I know how to look at numbers, I f*cking love spreadsheets.

How many songs are on spotify? **over 100 million songs**. How many songs are added weekly? **thousands**. And then how does someone stand out in a world like that especially when their entire lyrical rhythm and flow sounds like an NF clone? Answer: *you don't*. 

## The Plan
I need to develop a plan to stick to and follow. If I just try to keep writing songs willy-nilly, this isn't going to work. The creative process is dead, and I don't have very much money to buy a defribilator to bring it back to life, but I can maybe do a little CPR.
