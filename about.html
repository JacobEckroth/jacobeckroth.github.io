Work Experience

I currently have two jobs within Oregon State University. My first position is as an Engineering Ambassador. We represent the College of Engineering in outreach and recruitment, we engage with alumni and industry partners, and we visit highschools and assist with orientation.


My second position at OSU is serving as a SafeRide driver. SafeRide is a service offered by ASOSU, the associated student body at Oregon State. It is essentially a shuttle service that operates from 8 PM - 2 AM weekdays and weeknights. We drive people who might not be able to drive themselves, or who don't feel safe driving, around Corvallis and the surrounding areas back to their homes.
